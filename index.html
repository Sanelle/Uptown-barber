Our Story

Founded in 2015 by master barber James Wilson, UpTown Barber was born from a passion for traditional barbering with a modern twist. What started as a single-chair shop in Woodstock has grown into Cape Town's premier grooming destination.

We believe that a great haircut is more than just a service—it's an experience. Our barbers are trained in both classic techniques and contemporary styles, ensuring you leave looking and feeling your best.
